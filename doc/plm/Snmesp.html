Employment and Wages in Spain

Description

A panel of 738 observations from 1983 to 1990

total number of observations: 5904

observation: firms

country: Spain

Usage

data(Snmesp)

Format

A data frame containing:

firm

firm index

year

year

n

log of employment

w

log of wages

y

log of real output

i

log of intermediate inputs

k

log of real capital stock

f

real cash flow

Source

Journal of Business Economics and Statistics data archive:

http://www.amstat.org/publications/jbes/.

References

Alonso–Borrego, C. and Arellano, M. (1999). Symmetrically Normalized Instrumental-Variable Estimation Using Panel Data, Journal of Business and Economic Statistics, 17(1), 36–49.
